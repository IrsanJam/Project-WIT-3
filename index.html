What is Biji Ketapang and Benefits

What is Biji Ketapang and Benefits, Biji ketapang is one of the traditional pastries typical of the Betawi people. This cake is made from basic ingredients of wheat flour, margarine, sugar, coconut milk, eggs, salt, and vanilla. The most frequent benefits of biji ketapang utilized is its ability to lower cholesterol. If not treated immediately the risk of a heart attack is higher.
